New Store Item: Field Notes Memo Books

July 25, 2013 | Posted in CLC | Keywords: store

Purchase at the CLC store

The hottest notebook since the marble composition book! Field Notes are made in America, fit easily in your purse or pocket, and are inspired by vintage agricultural memo books and ledgers. They are perfect to bring along on a walk to keep track of your tracks! Use as a hiking diary. List birds, animals and plants encountered during your adventures. Or jot down a few lines of verse inspired by your forays into nature. They are also great for grocery and to-do lists, directions and recipes.

Memo Books are available in three distinct designs: Classic Kraft Paper cover, Limited Edition America the Beautiful series with photo covers, and New York State County Fair books brimming with facts about our beloved state. Each book measures 3-1/2” wide by 5-1/2” tall and is bound with a rugged three-staple saddle-stitch process. All styles come in lined, blank, or graph paper.

Purchase at the CLC store for $3 each, or 3 for $8 mix and match designs and paper styles.

Local Walkbook Republished

December 15, 2011 | Posted in CLC, Headline, Publication | Keywords: easements, store

In honor of our 25th Anniversary, CLC is reissuing and updating the popular book, Walks by Protected Land by Sheldon Evans. The updated walk guide includes 20 walks on public roads passing by land permanently protected by conservation easements in Columbia County. The walks range from 3.8 to 7 miles. The guide includes a description of each walk, detailed directions with mile markers, and a topographic map.

Large tracts of conserved land are important for wildlife habitat and biodiversity. These walks feature some of the areas most treasured protected lands, several of which are over 1,000 acres. The walks offer great views of diverse landscapes and a chance to peacefully connect with the natural world.

The book sells for $16, including tax, and is available at the CLC store at 49 Main Street in Chatham, NY as well as local bookstores. All proceeds from the book support CLC.

CLC Nature Store Opens Friday

November 22, 2011 | Posted in CLC | Keywords: store

CLC is opening up a store that sells products that will help people enjoy and appreciate working lands, scenic lands, and wildlife habitats. Items include binoculars, field guides, locally made jewelry and crafts, ecologically sustainable coffee, and note cards featuring local sites.

We are selling items that reflect our mission – to connect people to the land. We are offering seasonal, local, and nature related products. All of the proceeds from the store will support CLC’s programs and conservation efforts.
