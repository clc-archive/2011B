Previous CLC Events at Hawthorne Valley:

Edible Wild Plant Walk

Sunday, June 5th , 2011 1:00pm

Event is Full – no more participants
Russ Cohen, professional environmentalist and wild foods enthusiast, leads an outdoor walk and introduces 25 – 45 different species of edible wild plants, some in collectible quantity and condition. Keys to the identification of each species will be provided, as well as information about its edible portion(s), season(s) of availability, flavor and nutritive value, and tips on preparation. Cohen will also discuss guidelines for safe and environmentally responsible foraging. Participants may find it useful to bring a jackknife, several bags for collecting samples, notebook, and pen.

Bill McKibben

Tuesday, June 21st , 2011 5:30pm

Free, suggested donation

In honor of CLC’s 25th Anniversary, Hawthorne Valley is hosting renowned author, educator, and environmentalist Bill McKibben for an afternoon engagement.  Don’t miss this opportunity to hear McKibben’s take on our changing planet and how you can make a difference while enjoying local produced refreshments on one of CLC’s earliest conservation easements.

At 4:30 pm there will be a pre-event reception with drinks and light fare. Charge for the pre-event reception is $40.

To make reservations or for more information call 518.672.5808. Co-sponsored by Hawthorne Valley Farm with FarmAssist Productions.
