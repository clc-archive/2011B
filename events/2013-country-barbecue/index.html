2013 Country Barbecue

Sunday, May 26th 4:30pm – 8:00pm

Clum And Patchen Farm – Platner Road, Livingston, NY

Attend CLC’s annual fundraiser and celebration of rural culture with live entertainment, activities for children of all ages, and delicious local food and beverages.

BEAUTIFUL LOCATION

This year, CLC’s Country Barbecue returns to David Rockefeller’s Clum and Patchen Farm in Livingston, NY. A long drive lined with poplar trees leads to 400 acres of prime farmland with tremendous Catskill views. From the parking area you can ride on a horse drawn trolley for additional views.

NON-STOP ENTERTAINMENT

The Country Barbecue is packed with a diverse range of entertainment drawing from rural traditions.

  • Acclaimed Country Western Band The Spurs USA
  • Bindlestiff Family Cirkus
  • Animals: Birds of Prey, Petting Farm, Working Oxen
  • Hands-On Activities for Children

LOCAL FOOD AND BEVERAGE

Food and beverages are served from 4:30pm until 7:30pm.
Delicious food is sourced from local farms and there will be local beer from award-winning Chatham Brewing. Highlights include grilled local chicken with a Scotch Ale Chipotle sauce, Mediterranean pasta salad, a variety of local gourmet cheeses, and assorted desserts.

SILENT AUCTION & RAFFLE

Bidding begins at 4:30pm and ends at 7:00pm
View raffle items and select silent auction items.

Casual dress, please wear sturdy shoes
Event is held rain or shine
