Country Barbecue Entertainment

April 19, 2012

Cathy Grier is performing at the Country Barbecue

Joining 1,000 supporters of local conservation at a beautiful Columbia County farm for a barbecue featuring local food, an array of wines and locally brewed beer is the perfect way to inaugurate the summer. Yet the Columbia Land Conservancy’s Country Barbecue is so much more. CLC’s main fundraising event, the barbecue is being held on Sunday, May 27 at David Rockefeller’s Clum and Patchen Farm and is packed with a diverse range of entertainment.

The fun begins in the parking area where you can take a ride on a horse drawn trolley, organized by Dreaming Tree Farm. If you want more, you can take another ride around the Rockefeller’s beautiful farm land. CONTINUE READING

Trout Released

April 3, 2012

Grab your fishing polls and head over to Siegel-Kline Kill Conservation Area. Working with the Columbia Land Conservancy and Trout Unlimited, the New York Department of Environmental Conservation (NYSDEC) introduced many trout into the creek at the Public Conservation Area. Fishing licenses can be obtained at most town clerk offices and sporting good stores.
