Irene Emergency Assistance for Farmers

September 6, 2011 | Posted in Non CLC Event, Working Farms | Keywords: disaster, hurricane

Thursday, September 8, 2011 at 3:00 PM
Emergency Service Center,
6 1/2 Station Road, Goshen, NY

FEMA, FSA (Farm Services Agency), Soil & Water Conservation Districts, Crop Insurance representation, Cornell, Farm Credit will discuss funding sources/agency assistance available for recovery from Irene specific to agricultural producers. There will also be some discussion on steps you can take to recover your fields/crops for the future.

Farmers from any NY county are welcome to attend since the vast majority of the information will be applicable, especially if their county has the same disaster designation as Orange.

FSA has a 15 day notice-of-loss time limit.

Economic Impact of Beginning Farmers

August 19, 2011 | Posted in Non CLC Event, Working Farms

Economic Impact of Beginning Farmers – Small but Mighty
What language and numbers are needed to advocate with policy-makers and others who dismiss the economic impact of many start-up farms?
Many have expressed a desire to have numbers they can use–beyond all the good feel-good stories of new farms–to defend and advocate for their contributions to the economic vibrancy of their areas. Anu Rangarajan and Matthew Goldfarb from the Small Farms Program took this request to heart, and have spent time this Summer surveying the literature, meeting with economic development specialists, and steeping themselves in the world of economic development.
