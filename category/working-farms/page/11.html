Spring 2012 Online Courses for Beginning Farmers

January 17, 2012 | Posted in Non CLC Event, Working Farms

This Spring Cornell Small Farms Program is offering 4 online courses.

Courses are taught by experienced Cooperative Extension educators, farmers, and other specialists. Courses run 5-8 weeks, cost $175, and include both real-time meetings (online webinars) and on-your-own time reading and activities. They do not offer any academic credit, but those who successfully complete a course will receive a certificate and are also eligible for Farm Service Agency (FSA) borrower training credit, which can improve your eligibility to receive a low-interest FSA loan.
