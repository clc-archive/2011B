Farm Film Fest Call for Entries

December 2, 2011 | Posted in Arts & Entertainment, CLC, Headline, Working Farms | Keywords: movie


The festival shows films that focus on farms, farming, and farming issues and is especially interested in films that have a local connection. Films should be from 5-20 minutes and submitted in DVD format. The program includes films made by professional and amateur filmmakers. Students of all ages are encouraged to submit films. Selection of films for the festival is done by a panel of local filmmakers and farmers. Deadline for submissions is January 20, 2012.

DVDs can be sent to:
Farm Film Fest IV
The Chatham Film Club
PO Box 181
East Chatham, NY 12060

Siegel-Kline Kill Stocked With Trout

May 2, 2011 | Posted in Arts & Entertainment, CLC, Headline


Grab your fishing polls and head over to Siegel-Kline Kill Conservation Area. Working with the Columbia Land Conservancy and Trout Unlimited, the New York Department of Environmental Conservation (NYSDEC) introduced fifty brown trout into the creek at the public conservation area. Fishing licenses can be obtained at most town clerk offices and sporting good stores.
