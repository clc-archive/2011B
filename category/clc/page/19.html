Farm Film Fest Call for Entries

December 2, 2011 | Posted in Arts & Entertainment, CLC, Headline, Working Farms | Keywords: movie


The festival shows films that focus on farms, farming, and farming issues and is especially interested in films that have a local connection. Films should be from 5-20 minutes and submitted in DVD format. The program includes films made by professional and amateur filmmakers. Students of all ages are encouraged to submit films. Selection of films for the festival is done by a panel of local filmmakers and farmers. Deadline for submissions is January 20, 2012.

DVDs can be sent to:
Farm Film Fest IV
The Chatham Film Club
PO Box 181
East Chatham, NY 12060

CLC Nature Store Opens Friday

November 22, 2011 | Posted in CLC | Keywords: store

CLC is opening up a store that sells products that will help people enjoy and appreciate working lands, scenic lands, and wildlife habitats. Items include binoculars, field guides, locally made jewelry and crafts, ecologically sustainable coffee, and note cards featuring local sites.

We are selling items that reflect our mission – to connect people to the land. We are offering seasonal, local, and nature related products. All of the proceeds from the store will support CLC’s programs and conservation efforts.

National Census of Land Trusts

November 16, 2011 | Posted in CLC, Headline, Publication | Keywords: easements, land trust alliance

Private land conservation efforts produced dramatic results in the years between 2005 and 2010. A census released by the Land Trust Alliance reports that the owners of 10 million acres of land nationwide chose to permanently protect their properties in that time, including 973,807 acres here in New York.  The National Land Trust Census shows that voluntarily protected private land increased 27% between 2005 and 2010. In New York, the increase was 88%. In the same time period the federal Land and Water Conservation Fund, a major federal conservation program, added just over 500,000 acres and saw a 38% funding cut.
