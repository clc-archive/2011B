I design and develop websites with attention to W3C standards, accessibility, and the ever-changing best practices of the day. I work with HTML and CSS (including HTML5 & CSS3), JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, Wordpress, and Drupal. Based on my marketing experience as well as my work in other media, I understand the need to have websites complement other pieces of a media strategy.

By analyzing user engagement, I can improve functionality and a website's ability to achieve goals. While running clctrust.org, visits increased by 46%, page loads by 60%, and online donations by 57% compared to the prior year.
