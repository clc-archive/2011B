Hello all, I want to get your materials up on our website -- PowerPoints, handouts, references to documents that were handed out in limited number that day, etc.  Let's dicuss this on Thursday.  If your documents are too large, we can use Dropbox or something similar.
 
Meanwhile, here are some numbers for Saturday's workshop.  They look good!:
  • There were 37 attending, not counting Peter Paden or HREP staff.  That's the second lowest attendance we've had, which I attribute to the Saturday schedule.  Our one other Saturday workshop had 34 attending, whereas the Conservation Advisory Council workshop had 57 or 58, and Stormwater had 44.  We received 34 evaluations, however, and the largest percentage ever -- a good marker.
  • There was a good variety:  (15) planning board members, (8) zoning board, and two each of town board members, CAC members, EMC members, and four residents who didn't claim   Several attending had two or three affiliations.  Their range of experience was also very broad, but leaned toward greater experience, with 24 attendees claiming at least several years involvement in their work to professional level experience.  Ten people were new (6) or had just over one year experience (4.)
  • (21) attending said the workshop "met" expectations and (14) said "exceeded" expectations.  This compares well against the CAC workshop (33 and 12, respectively.)  A total of 24 said that the workshop increased their knowledge to at least one greater level.  One with "considerable" knowledget still felt the workshop "exceeded" expectations.  The same number said they'd be able to use the knowledge in two or more ways in their town.
